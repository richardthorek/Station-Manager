# Reports

*Requires the Basic plan or above.*

Reports turn the sign-in book and truck-check history into answers: who's
turning up, what activities get attended, whether the trucks are being
checked, and where the trends are heading.

## The reports dashboard

Open **Reports** from the home screen. The main dashboard shows:

- **Attendance summary** — check-ins over time, busiest days, and
  participation by activity type (training vs maintenance vs meetings).
- **Member participation** — who's attending what, hours at the station,
  engagement patterns (including a heat map of when the station is busy).
- **Truck-check compliance** — how consistently each vehicle is being
  checked, and outstanding issues.
- **KPI cards and insights** — headline numbers with plain-language
  callouts.

Use the date-range picker to focus on a month, quarter, or custom period.

## Advanced and cross-station reports

- **Advanced reports** (`Reports → Advanced`) — deeper breakdowns and
  visualisations for the analytically inclined.
- **Cross-station reports** (`Reports → Cross-station`) — for organisations
  with more than one station: compare attendance and compliance across
  stations side by side.

## Exporting data

Anything you can report on, you can take with you:

- **CSV export** — attendance and truck-check results export to CSV for
  spreadsheets or brigade records. Truck-check exports support a date-range
  filter (Vehicle Check admin dashboard → History tab → Export CSV).
- **PDF / image export** — report views can be exported for pasting into a
  brigade meeting deck or annual report.

## Who can see reports?

Reports need a login or a linked kiosk — they're not publicly visible. Anyone
signed in under your organisation (owner, admin, or invited member) can view
them; the kiosk tablet can too via its brigade access token.
