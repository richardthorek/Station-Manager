# Profiles & Achievements

## Your member profile

Tap your name's profile link (or scan your QR code) to open your profile page.
It shows:

- **Your QR code** — scan it at the station to check in instantly. Print it
  and stick it in your helmet, wallet, or on your locker.
- **Your stats** — check-ins, hours at the station, activity breakdown
  (training vs maintenance vs meetings), and recent history.
- **Your achievements** — badges you've earned (see below).

Admins additionally see management actions on a profile, including **Invite to
login** (sends a one-time activation link so the member can set a password) and
editing member details.

## Achievements

Station Manager includes an optional, just-for-fun achievement system to
encourage participation. You earn badges automatically by showing up and
pitching in — attending training, doing maintenance, coming to meetings,
completing truck checks, and keeping up streaks.

There are **20 achievements** across five categories — Training, Maintenance,
Meetings, Patterns (like *Wednesday Warrior* and *Early Bird*), and Milestones
(like *Perfect Month*) — in Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers.

See the full list and how each one is earned: **[Achievements reference](achievements.md)**.

Progress is calculated from your check-in history, so there's nothing to set
up — just sign in when you're at the station and the badges take care of
themselves.
