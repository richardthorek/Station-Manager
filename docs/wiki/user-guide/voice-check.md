# Voice Check (AI assistant)

*Requires the AI Pro plan.*

Voice check lets you do a truck check **hands-free** — walk around the truck
and talk, and the assistant records the results into the same checklist and
history as a manual check. It knows your truck: its type's standard checklist,
your brigade's custom items, its walk-around zones and equipment, and any
quirks notes your admin has recorded.

## Starting a voice check

1. Open **Vehicle Check** and tap the **🎙 Voice Check** button on the
   vehicle's card (it only appears on AI Pro).
2. Allow **microphone access** when the browser asks — the assistant can't
   hear you without it.
3. Wait for the status pill to show **Ready**.

## Talking to the assistant

- **Hold the big button while you speak**, let go when you're done — like a
  radio. What you said appears in the transcript, and the assistant replies
  out loud (and in text).
- Speak naturally: *"Starting on the Cat 1"*, *"tyres are all good"*, *"the
  left rear locker light's not working"*, *"what's next?"*.
- The assistant works through the checklist **zone by zone** in walk-around
  order, tells you when it changes areas, and reads back any issue it records
  so you can correct it on the spot.
- When everything's recorded and you confirm you're done, it closes off the
  check and says goodbye. A **View check summary** link takes you to the same
  summary page as a manual check.

**Prefer typing?** There's a text box under the talk button — the assistant
answers the same way. Handy in a quiet room or if the mic is playing up.

## Good to know

- **It records real results.** A voice check produces the same auditable
  check record as a manual one — same history, same issue follow-ups, same
  reports. A full transcript of the conversation is also kept.
- **Joining in:** if a check is already underway on that truck (manual or
  voice), the assistant adds your results to it rather than starting a
  duplicate.
- **Dropouts:** if the connection drops (shed Wi-Fi…), the app reconnects by
  itself and picks up the same session, restoring the conversation so far.
  The status pill shows **Reconnecting…** while it does.
- **No sound on an iPad?** iPads only allow sound after you've touched the
  screen — press the talk button once and replies will play. If playback is
  blocked the reply is always shown as text, so you never lose the answer.
- **Limits:** sessions have sensible caps (length of a single utterance,
  number of turns) to keep things responsive; the assistant will tell you if
  you hit one.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to do |
|---|---|
| "Microphone unavailable" | Check the browser's mic permission for the site (padlock icon in the address bar), and that nothing else is using the mic |
| Replies show as text but no audio | Tap the talk button once (unlocks audio on iPad), check the volume/silent switch |
| Status stuck on Reconnecting… | Check the internet connection; the app retries several times, then you can reopen the page — your progress is saved |
| "Not in your plan" | Voice check needs the AI Pro plan — see the [administrator guide](admin-guide.md) |
